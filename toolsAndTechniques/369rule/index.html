The 369 Manifestation Technique

  • 1. Identify Your Goal
    Decide on a clear, specific goal you want to achieve. It could be related to your studies, personal growth, or any other area of your life.
  • 2. Write It Down
    • Morning (3 times): As soon as you wake up, write your goal or affirmation down three times. For example, "I am confident and successful in my studies."
    • Afternoon (6 times): Around midday, write your goal or affirmation six times. This keeps your mind focused on what you want.
    • Evening (9 times): Before you go to bed, write your goal or affirmation nine times. This helps imprint it in your subconscious mind.
  • 3. Be Consistent
    Do this every day for at least 21 days. Consistency is key to making this technique work.
  • 4. Visualize and Feel
    While writing your goal, visualize achieving it. Feel the emotions you would experience if your goal were already a reality.
  • 5. Stay Positive
    Keep a positive attitude and believe in the process. Trust that your goal is on its way to becoming a reality. By following these steps, you can harness the power of the 369 Manifestation Technique to achieve your goals and create positive changes in your life.
