Welcome!

Is emotional pain, shame, and struggle getting in the way of living the life you really want?
Would you like to change unwanted behaviors, feelings, and improve the quality of your relationships?
I’m Danielle, a licensed psychotherapist in Northern California and I am here to help. It’s often overwhelming trying to find the right therapist, especially when you aren’t feeling your best. I’ve been there.
I hope that this site can give you a feel for who I am and what our work together will be like. If you think we might be a good fit, I encourage you to reach out and schedule a phone consultation with me, free of charge.
I work with clients in person at my San Rafael office, as well as offering convenient Online Therapy.
